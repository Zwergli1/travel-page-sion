Why Sion?

Sion, the capital of wine in the heart of the Alps

Dominated by its two emblematic castles, Valère and Tourbillon, Sion is the Swiss capital of the Alps. The town stands out for its dynamism, friendliness, sunny climate and rich historical and archaeological heritage. So many assets that make it a quality destination.

In the heart of the Alps, its territory of some 35 km2 stretches from plain to mountain, reaching an altitude of 2300 meters. With 420 hectares of vineyards, Sion is the second-largest wine-growing commune in the Valais.
